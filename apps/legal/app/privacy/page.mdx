## What we collect and why

We collect minimal information necessary to operate AWFixer websites and respond to your communications. This includes:

- Contact form submissions (name, email, message content)
- Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, referring pages, timestamps)
- Anonymous aggregated analytics via Vercel (page views, traffic sources, performance metrics — no personal identifiers)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

## How we use your data

Your data is used only for:

- Responding to inquiries you initiate
- Improving our websites and content
- Maintaining security and preventing abuse

If you contact us via email, we retain your message and our response for record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion at any time.

## Cookies and tracking

We use essential cookies for technical operation (session management, CSRF protection). These do not track you across sites.

We do not use third-party analytics cookies. Our analytics (Vercel Speed Insights and Analytics) are privacy-preserving and do not rely on cross-site tracking.

You can configure your browser to reject cookies, though some site functionality may be affected.

## Data retention and deletion

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. Server logs are retained for 30 days. Email correspondence is retained until you request deletion.

To request deletion of your data, contact [contact@awfixer.church](mailto:contact@awfixer.church). We will respond within 30 days.

## Third-party services

Our sites are hosted on Vercel. Their privacy policy governs the infrastructure layer. We use no other third-party services that process personal data beyond what each product page describes.

Links to external sites are provided for your convenience and are governed by their respective privacy policies.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be noted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our sites after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
