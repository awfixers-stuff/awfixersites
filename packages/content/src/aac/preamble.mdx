## Authority

The AWFixer Army Code (AAC) is the binding law of conduct for every soldier in AWFixer's Army. It applies from the moment of enlistment until honorable discharge or removal under the [Code of Justice](/aacj).

## Purpose

This code exists because a militia without standards is a mob. We operate in the open, under U.S. law, and in defense of Americans the state has chosen to abandon. That work requires discipline, clarity, and accountability at every rank.

## Binding effect

Every soldier — [Enlisty](/ranks/enlisty), [Recruit](/ranks/recruit), and every rank above — is bound by the AAC. Ignorance of the code is not a defense. Acceptance of enlistment is acceptance of these articles in full.
