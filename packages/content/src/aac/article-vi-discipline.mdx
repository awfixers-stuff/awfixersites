## Enforcement

The AAC is not a suggestion document. Violations are investigated and adjudicated under the [AWFixer Army Code of Justice](/aacj).

## Reporting

Any soldier who witnesses a breach of the AAC must report it to their immediate superior or, if the superior is implicated, to the next rank above.

## Standards at enlistment

Soldiers who cannot meet the unit's standards will be discharged before they can damage the mission. See [enlistment standards](/enlist) for entry requirements.

## Relation to the AACJ

This article does not define offenses or penalties — those are set forth in the AACJ. The AAC defines what is required. The AACJ defines what happens when requirements are broken.
