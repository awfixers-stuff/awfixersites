## Classification

Operational details — locations, identities, timelines, and methods — are restricted by default. Assume everything you know about an active or planned operation is sensitive until released.

## Prohibited disclosure

- Sharing operational plans, routes, or identities of protected persons outside authorized channels.
- Posting militia business on public social media without clearance.
- Discussing investigations, tribunals, or pending charges except as required by the AACJ.

## Secure channels

Use only unit-approved communication methods for sensitive material. Personal devices and unsecured apps are not authorized for operational traffic.

## Records

Official communications are retained. Deletion, alteration, or concealment of records is an offense under the [AACJ](/aacj).
