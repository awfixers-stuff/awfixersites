## Training before deployment

No soldier deploys on a live operation without completing training appropriate to their specialty and assignment. Readiness is not optional.

## Rules of engagement

- Use force only when necessary to protect life, complete a lawful extraction, or defend assigned positions.
- Minimize harm to civilians and non-combatants.
- Document engagements, injuries, and material used in official records.

## Extraction and relocation

- Verify identity and consent where operationally possible before moving protected persons.
- Maintain chain-of-custody for persons, documents, and supplies under unit control.
- Do not abandon a protected party once extraction has begun except on lawful order.

## After action

Every operation produces a written after-action report filed through the chain of command. Lessons learned are not optional decoration — they keep the next team alive.
