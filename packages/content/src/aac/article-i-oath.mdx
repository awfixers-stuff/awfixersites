## The oath

Upon enlistment, every soldier swears allegiance to the mission: protect Americans at risk of being discarded, displaced, or erased — and to obey lawful orders issued through the chain of command.

## Requirements

- Place the mission above personal gain, politics, or ego.
- Refuse unlawful orders and report them through proper channels.
- Protect civilians under the unit's care, even at personal cost.
- Speak truth in all official records, reports, and testimony.

## Breach

Violation of this article is grounds for charges under [Article II — Offenses](/aacj/article-ii-offenses) of the AACJ.
