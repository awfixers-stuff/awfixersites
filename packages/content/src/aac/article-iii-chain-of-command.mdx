## Structure

Authority flows from the [Commander in Chief](/ranks/commander-in-chief) down through [General](/ranks/general), [Captain](/ranks/captain), [Sergeant](/ranks/sergeant), to every soldier at the bottom of the chain.

## Orders

- Lawful orders from a superior must be obeyed without delay.
- Soldiers may request clarification; they may not substitute their own judgment for a lawful order in the field.
- Disputes between ranks are resolved up the chain, not sideways in public.

## Communication

- Report status, risks, and completed actions to your immediate superior.
- Do not bypass the chain except to report unlawful orders or imminent danger that cannot wait.
- Sponsors remain accountable for recruits they brought into the unit.
