## Standard

A soldier represents the militia at all times. Bearing, speech, and action must reflect the seriousness of the work.

## On duty

- Follow orders promptly and completely unless they are unlawful.
- Maintain equipment, readiness, and assigned specialty competence.
- Treat fellow soldiers and protected civilians with respect and restraint.

## Off duty

- Do not conduct that brings disrepute upon the unit or compromises operations.
- Do not use the militia's name, insignia, or resources for personal business.
- Do not engage in conduct that would disqualify enlistment under current [standards](/enlist).

## Public representation

When speaking as a soldier of AWFixer's Army, speak plainly and truthfully. Do not speculate about active operations. Do not speak for the unit without authorization.
