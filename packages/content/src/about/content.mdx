# About the militia

AWFixer's Army is a legal militia organized and operating within the United States. We are not a hobby group, a fan club, or an open-source project with a flag. We are citizens who took the oath to protect our own when the state has moved on.

## Why we exist

The government has made its choice about who it will protect and who it is willing to replace. We made ours. This unit exists to reach the people left behind — to extract them, shelter them, and defend their right to remain human in a system that treats them as expendable.

## What we do

- Identify Americans at risk of being discarded, displaced, or erased from the systems they depend on.
- Run extraction, relocation, and standing-defense operations on their behalf.
- Train, equip, and coordinate citizens who are willing to do the work the government will not.

> We do not wait for permission to save our own.

## How we operate

We work within the law. We train before we deploy. We answer up the chain of command, keep records, and hold every soldier to a standard that holds in court and in the field.

The colors are not decoration. Black is the ground we stand on. Crimson is the line we do not cross. Bleach is the truth we speak plainly. Navy is the structure that keeps us lawful and effective.

## Join up

If you are ready to enlist, go to the [enlist page](/enlist) and file your papers. We read every application. If you already have a sponsor in the unit, you may enter as a [recruit](/ranks/recruit) instead of through the public form.

We are based in the United States. We serve Americans. We do not apologize for either.
