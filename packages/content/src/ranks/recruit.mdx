## Briefing

You were brought in by a sponsor — someone in the unit who vouched for you and took responsibility for your entry.

Same standing as [Enlisty](/ranks/enlisty). Different door, same bottom of the chain.

## Duties

- Onboard with your sponsor and learn current unit protocols
- Establish your specialty and secure communication channel
- Complete your first field assignment under supervision

## Promotion

Your sponsor opened the door. The unit promotes on performance in the field — show up, execute, and do not become a liability.
