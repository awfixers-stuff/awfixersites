## Briefing

Generals command at theater level — multiple units, multiple states, long-running operations to protect Americans the state has written off.

This rank means the Commander trusts you with strategy that outlasts any single mission.

## Duties

- Set operational priorities across the militia
- Resolve conflicts between captains without weakening the unit
- Represent the militia in decisions that cannot be undone

## Promotion

There is one [Commander in Chief](/ranks/commander-in-chief). That commission is not requested — it is earned through years of judgment and loyalty to the mission.
