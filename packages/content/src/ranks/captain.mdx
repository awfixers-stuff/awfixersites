## Briefing

Captains own operations — extraction routes, defense postures, timelines, and the people under their command. When a captain gives the order, the unit moves.

## Duties

- Plan and execute missions to protect Americans the government has abandoned
- Coordinate sergeants and soldiers across multiple workstreams
- Write after-action reports that survive scrutiny — legal and operational

## Promotion

Generals think across regions and months, not single deployments. Demonstrate that you can hold multiple captains aligned without losing the mission.
