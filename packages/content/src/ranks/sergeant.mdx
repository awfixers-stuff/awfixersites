## Briefing

Sergeants have proven themselves in the field. They lead small units, run drills, and are trusted to hold a position when things go wrong.

You are still on the line — but now the people beside you are your responsibility.

## Duties

- Lead squad-level operations and keep your unit coordinated
- Train enlisties and recruits to unit standard
- Report up the chain with accuracy — no exaggeration, no omission

## Promotion

Captains are made from sergeants who can run an entire operation without someone holding their hand.
