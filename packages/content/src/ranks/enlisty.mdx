## Briefing

You filed through the public enlistment form. That matters — you stepped forward without a sponsor and put your name on the list.

[Recruit](/ranks/recruit) came in through someone already in the unit. Day one, you are equals.

## Duties

- Complete enlistment and report to your assigned unit
- Complete basic readiness training before any field assignment
- Prove you can follow orders, keep your mouth shut, and show up when called

## Promotion

Sergeants are made from soldiers who are reliable under pressure — not loud, not political, present when it counts.
