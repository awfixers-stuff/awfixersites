## Categories

Offenses are grouped by severity. This article establishes categories; specific charges are filed under one or more categories with supporting facts.

### Minor offenses

- Failure to follow lawful orders without immediate operational justification
- Neglect of assigned duties or equipment
- Unauthorized disclosure of non-sensitive but official information

### Major offenses

- Breach of [Article V — Communications and OPSEC](/aac/article-v-communications)
- Falsification of records or after-action reports
- Abandonment of an extraction or protected party without lawful order
- Conduct that would have barred enlistment under current [standards](/enlist)

### Capital offenses

- Betrayal of operational security resulting in harm to protected persons or soldiers
- Violence against protected civilians under unit care
- Willful sabotage of the mission or the chain of command

> Severity guides sanction range. Aggravating facts may elevate a charge.
