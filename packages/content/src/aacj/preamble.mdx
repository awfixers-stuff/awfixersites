## Authority

The AWFixer Army Code of Justice (AACJ) is the militia's system of military justice. It governs investigation, charges, tribunals, sanctions, and appeals for every soldier bound by the [AWFixer Army Code](/aac).

## Purpose

A unit that saves lives cannot tolerate soldiers who endanger them. The AACJ exists to protect the mission, the protected, and the soldiers who execute the work — by removing bad actors quickly and fairly.

## Fair process

Accusations are documented. The accused is heard. Findings are recorded. Sanctions are proportional. Appeals exist because justice must be seen to be done, not merely declared.
