## Persons subject

The AACJ applies to every enlisted soldier and every rank in the chain of command, from [Enlisty](/ranks/enlisty) through [Commander in Chief](/ranks/commander-in-chief).

## Conduct covered

Jurisdiction extends to:

- Acts that violate the [AAC](/aac)
- Conduct on duty, off duty, or in any context that affects the unit or its operations
- Acts committed during training, deployment, or official militia business

## Geographic scope

The militia operates within the United States. The AACJ applies wherever a soldier acts in an official or representational capacity on behalf of AWFixer's Army.

## Limitations

The AACJ does not replace civilian criminal process where applicable law requires referral to civil authorities. It runs in parallel where the unit must act faster than the state will.
