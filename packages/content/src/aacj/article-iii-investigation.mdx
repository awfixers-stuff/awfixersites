## Initiation

Any soldier may file a report. Superiors who receive credible allegations must open an investigation or escalate within 24 hours.

## Investigation standards

- Identify witnesses, documents, and communications relevant to the allegation.
- Preserve evidence in original form where possible.
- Avoid public discussion of pending matters except as required for safety.

## Charges

When investigation supports probable cause, the investigating officer forwards a charge sheet to tribunal authority with:

- Named accused and rank
- Article(s) alleged
- Factual summary and evidence list
- Recommended severity classification

## Rights of the accused

The accused is notified of charges in writing, provided the evidence against them, and given reasonable time to prepare a defense before tribunal.
